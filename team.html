Director

Research Interests: Digital Twin, AI, XR, Wearable Devices, Human Factor

Dr. Sakib is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (courtesy) at UTA. His research goal is to apply multidisciplinary knowledge and skillset to understand and advance the human-technology frontier to benefit future development towards a sustainable and resilient AEC industry.
